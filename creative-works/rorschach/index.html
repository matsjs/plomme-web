Rorschach

I got a bit obsessed with the Rorschach/ inkblot patterns after watching and reading the Watchmen comic book/ movie, so I had to recreate the changing mask. I use simplex noise here for performance and just mirror it for some cool patterns. Watch it long enough and you will see a butterfly - or your repressed childhood memories.
